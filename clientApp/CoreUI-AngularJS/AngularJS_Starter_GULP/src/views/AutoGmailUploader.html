Auto Invoice Upload form

Here you can add your email details and select your preferences for using this tool.

This tool automatically searches in your emails and downloads all the relevent invoices

Note: This tool only works with Gmail

Instructions

1. Go to your Google account

2. On that page, go to "Less secure app access" and turn this feature on.

3. Now in the form below enter your Email address and email account password

4. Agree to the terms and conditions

5. If doing a full search for invoices in the gmail, then select the start date and finish date to search

6. If you want to use the weekly search and upload feature select it in preferences

7. Click submit, and the invoices will be downloaded and processed automatically.
